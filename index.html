TIMELINE

Here's a timeline of Thomas Sankara's life:

  • 1949 - Born in Yako, Upper Volta (Today's Burkina Faso).
  • 1973 - Graduated from the military academy.
  • 1974 - Become a national hero after the border war with Mali, a neighbouring country.
  • 1976 - While commanding the Commando Training Center, he urged the soldiers to help the civilians in their work tasks.
  • 1978 - Thomas Sankara organized the communist group in the Army.
  • 1981 - He became Secratary of State.
  • 1983 - He became President of the Upper Volta, which he rename 'Burkina Faso', meaning 'The republic of Honorable People'.
  • 1983 - 1987 - Thomas Sankara developped Rural's areas, Education and Helathcare. He supported 'Women's rights' and fought corruption and deforestation.
  • 1987 - Thomas Sankara was killed in a coup d'etat instigated by his former political ally, Blaise Compaore.
